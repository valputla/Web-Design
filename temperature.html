Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

The clustered data shown above and linear regression model utilized in the API project, suggests a strong correlation between latitude & maximum temperature of a city. The higher the latitude, or the more north you travel, the colder it gets. Or, the closer one travels to the equator, the hotter it becomes.
